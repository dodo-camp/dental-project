Implantologist & Cosmetic Dentistry

Dr. Vishal Gupta graduated with honors from Pune University in 1999. He procured Masters Degree in Orthodontics with the highest accolades in Post graduate studies, as he was conferred a gold medal and was adjudged the best outgoing student in the university. The last 7 years saw Dr.Gupta towering numerous advance dental workshops in Australia, USA & India.
He's been a part of various Orthodontic Conferences and Workshops at the National & International level. He is currently the Executive Member of the Indian Orthodontic Society. Dr. Gupta has received many awards and honors along his professional tenure.
